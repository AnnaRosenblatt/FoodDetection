Healthy Eating at School

Even when school food includes chicken tenders, tacos, chicken sandwiches, fries, and quesadillas, you can still make balanced choices.

Common School Foods

  • Chicken Tenders – good protein but often fried
  • Tacos – balanced if they include vegetables
  • Chicken Sandwich – protein and energy
  • Fries – best as a side food
  • Quesadillas – carbs and cheese for energy
