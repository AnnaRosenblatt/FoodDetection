Nutrition Basics

Important Nutrients

  • Protein helps your body grow and repair muscles
  • Carbohydrates provide energy
  • Healthy fats support brain function
  • Vitamins and minerals support the immune system
  • Water keeps your body hydrated and focused

Healthy Habits

  • Eat balanced meals
  • Do not skip breakfast
  • Drink water instead of sugary drinks
  • Pay attention to portion sizes
  • Try to include fruits or vegetables
